Why do we use .env?
To keep sensitive data (like database URIs) secure and configurable.

What does depends_on do?
Ensures services (e.g., backend) start only after their dependencies (mongo) are up.

What is useEffect in React?
A hook to fetch data or perform actions when a component loads or updates.
